About Me

I am currently pursuing a Doctor of Philosophy in Biomedical Engineering at Shenzhen University. My research primarily investigates the synthesis and applications of nanoparticles for various biomedical purposes. Previously, I earned my Master of Philosophy in Biotechnology from Mirpur University of Science and Technology and a Master of Science in Plant Sciences from Quaid-i-Azam University.
